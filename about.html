About Us

Our mission statement is to save the lives of the abandoned, injured, abused, sick, and neglected cats and kittens that find themselves in the high-kill Tipton county Animal Shelter as well as desperate cases in our community, one cat at a time. To find loving homes for each and every cat and kitten in our care, and to educate the community on proper care and the importance of spay/neuter.
